Classes Offered

Judo

Judo is a martial art that focuses on throws, pins, and joint locks, and originated in Japan. At DoBu, students can learn Judo from experienced instructors who can teach them the fundamentals of this martial art, as well as more advanced techniques. Learning Judo at DoBu can be beneficial for improving one's physical fitness, self-defense skills, and overall well-being.

Karate

Karate is a martial art that originated in Okinawa, Japan, and emphasizes striking techniques using hands, feet, and other parts of the body. DoBu offers Karate classes that are suitable for both beginners and advanced students. By learning Karate at DoBu, students can develop their self-confidence, discipline, and physical strength, while also improving their coordination and flexibility.

Join Now

Jiu-jitsu

Jiu-jitsu is a martial art that focuses on grappling techniques and ground fighting. At DoBu, students can learn Jiu-jitsu from experienced instructors who can help them develop their skills in self-defense, physical fitness, and mental discipline. Learning Jiu-jitsu can be beneficial for improving balance, coordination, flexibility, and overall health.

Join Now

Muay Thai

Muay Thai is a martial art that originated in Thailand and focuses on striking techniques using fists, elbows, knees, and shins. DoBu offers Muay Thai classes that are suitable for both beginners and advanced students, taught by experienced instructors. By learning Muay Thai at DoBu, students can improve their physical fitness, coordination, and self-defense skills, as well as their mental toughness and discipline

Kids Jiu-jitsu

Kids Jiu-jitsu is a specialized program at DoBu that teaches children the fundamentals of Jiu-jitsu in a safe and fun environment. By learning Kids Jiu-jitsu at DoBu, children can develop their coordination, balance, and confidence, while also learning important life skills such as discipline, respect, and perseverance.

Kids Karate

Kids Karate is another specialized program at DoBu that teaches children the basics of Karate in a fun and engaging way. By learning Kids Karate at DoBu, children can improve their physical fitness, coordination, and self-confidence, while also learning important values such as respect, self-discipline, and perseverance.

Private tuition

Private tuition sessions are personalized training sessions with one of our experienced instructors, where you can work on specific areas of your martial arts training. Whether you want to improve your technique, fitness, or overall performance, our instructors will design a program tailored to your individual needs and goals. Private tuition is a great way to accelerate your progress and take your skills to the next level.

Open mat/ personal practice

Open mat is a time for members to come in and train on their own or with a partner without a formal class structure. This time is great for working on individual techniques or simply getting some extra practice in. It's also an opportunity to connect with other members and build your network of training partners. Open mat is available to all members and is a great way to supplement your regular classes.
